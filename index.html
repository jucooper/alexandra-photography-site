A little bit about myself.

Photo by Sara Ormiston

My name is Alexandra Cooper and I started photography in 2017.

I originally bought a DSLR for traveling. My husband and I love going to national parks and visiting places in the US. Then I had this crazy idea of starting my own photography business. I’ve always had a creative side but my love for nature and animals drove me to study Biology in college. When I was taking pictures it was of birds, frogs, landscapes, and my pets.

I truly cannot explain the feeling when a client gets their photos back and knowing that I helped capture a moment they will hold on to forever. I love to see my photos improve with experience and self education. I want to always give my clients a service worth their money so I price my sessions fairly based on experience and quality of photos I provide.
